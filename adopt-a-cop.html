Adopt a Cop BJJ.

A nationwide nonprofit that pays 100% of an active-duty patrolling police officer's BJJ membership until they reach Blue Belt — typically 12 to 18 months of training. Mooresville BJJ is a proud affiliated academy.

Affiliated Academy · adoptacopbjj.org

For the men and women on patrol.

Mooresville Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a proud supporter of the men and women who put their lives on the line every day to keep us safe. That's why we're affiliated with the Adopt A Cop BJJ program.

How It Works

100% paid.
Until Blue Belt.

Adopt A Cop BJJ is a nonprofit, donation-based program that allows active-duty patrolling police officers to train at any affiliated academy and pays 100% of the officer's membership until they reach the rank of Blue Belt.

Blue Belt is the second-ranked belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and on average it takes around 12 to 18 months to obtain. Most individuals at blue-belt level have an immense advantage in controlling a combative situation over someone with no grappling knowledge — regardless of size, strength, or gender.

This is real, deployable skill that protects officers, suspects, and bystanders. Less force used, fewer injuries on both sides, and better outcomes when control is required.

For more information about the program, contact us or visit adoptacopbjj.org.

01

Who qualifies.

Active-duty patrolling police officers. Must be currently employed by a law enforcement agency. Federal, state, county, or municipal — all eligible.

02

What's covered.

100% of your membership at Mooresville BJJ. All Adult BJJ classes (Gi & No-Gi). All Open Mats. Funded entirely by Adopt A Cop BJJ donors and Mooresville BJJ.

03

How long.

From your first day until you earn Blue Belt — typically 12 to 18 months at 2-3 classes per week. The minute you hit Blue Belt, you can choose to continue at our standard membership rate.

04

How to apply.

Two paths: (1) Apply through adoptacopbjj.org, or (2) call (704) 360-0159 and we'll walk you through the process directly.
