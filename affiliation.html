Professor Tom DeBlass

4th Degree
Black Belt.

Tom DeBlass is a proud 4th Degree Black Belt student of Ricardo Almeida — a 6th Degree Black Belt under Renzo Gracie. Tom began his martial arts career studying Tae Kwon Do at age 6. In high school he was a county and state-sectional long jump champion.

Professor DeBlass has established himself as one of the best adult American Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu competitors. He is the ONLY person in NJ to win an adult Black Belt Pan American Title and the ONLY person in NJ history to win the North American ADCC trials twice.

His competition record includes medals and championships at the IBJJF Worlds, Nationals, and Pan American Jiu-Jitsu tournaments. Professor is also a UFC and Bellator Fighting Championship veteran.

Tom and his staff have built one of the largest BJJ academies in New Jersey — over 6,000 sq ft and close to 300 students.
