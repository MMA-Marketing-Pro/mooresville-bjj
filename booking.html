Home/Book a Trial Class

Claim your free class.

Pick a time below that works for you. Show up 10 minutes early in comfortable shorts and a clean T-shirt or rash guard — we'll take care of the rest. First class is on us. No contract. No sign-up fee.

What to expect

An instructor greets you 10 minutes before class. Light warm-up, partnered with a seasoned student, and we walk you through several new moves hands-on. You will never be asked to do anything you aren't comfortable with.

What to bring

Comfortable shorts (no zipper or rivets) and a clean T-shirt or rash guard. Water bottle. Bare feet on the mat — shoes off in the changing area. That's it for your first class.

Where to park

Free parking in front of the gym at 243 Overhill Drive. Look for the Mooresville BJJ sign on the building. Walk in 10 minutes early and an instructor will show you the changing area and the mat.

Need help booking or have questions? Call or text (704) 360-0159 or send us a message.
