Kids BJJ 5–7.

A playful, nurturing introduction to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for ages 5 to 7. Coordination, focus, and confidence — taught in a way that makes kids want to come back.

The Program

Coordination.
Confidence. Joy.

Our 5-7 class trains both Gi and No-Gi. Kids learn to move their bodies, listen to a coach, work with a partner, and follow simple, age-appropriate Jiu-Jitsu — all in a setting that feels more like play than drill.

At this age, technical mastery isn't the point. The point is building the habits — listening, focus, respect, persistence — that make every other thing in life easier. Jiu-Jitsu just happens to be the vehicle.

Every class is supervised by a black belt under Tom DeBlass. The room is supportive, the energy is high, and your child will leave tired in a good way.

Why Parents Pick Mooresville Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Built for little kids.
Not little adults.

Our 5-7 program is designed around how kids this age actually learn — short reps, lots of variety, plenty of encouragement, and zero pressure to be perfect.

01

Gi & No-Gi.

Kids learn both ways jiu-jitsu is trained. Plain white Gi for Gi class. Rash guard and shorts for No-Gi. Full kit available at the front desk.

02

Black-belt led.

Even the youngest class is supervised by a black belt. We don't hand kids off to a teenager with a few stripes — that's a difference you can feel in the first week.

03

Fun first.

Games, drills, and small partner work that teaches body awareness without ever feeling like a chore. Kids ask to come back. That's the metric we care about.

04

Confidence on and off the mat.

Parents tell us their kids stand a little taller after a few months — better posture, better eye contact, better at handling tough moments at school.

05

Real schedule.

5:00 PM Monday through Thursday, plus Saturday. Easy to fit around school and dinner. See the full schedule.

06

Free first class.
