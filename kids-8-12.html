Kids BJJ 8–12.

Discipline, self-defense, and real Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for ages 8 to 12. The kind of training that builds the kind of person you'd be proud to raise.

The Program

Real technique.
Real self-defense.

Our 8-12 class trains both Gi and No-Gi. At this age, kids are ready for real Jiu-Jitsu — escapes, takedowns, control positions, and the foundational submissions. Taught with structure, taught with care.

This is the age where Jiu-Jitsu compounds. Kids who start here arrive at high school with years of experience handling pressure, working with teammates, and standing up to the kind of physical conflicts that define the bullying years.

Black-belt led under Tom DeBlass. Lineage runs through Ricardo Almeida to Renzo Gracie. The teaching is real. The room is supportive. The results show up at school, at home, and on the mat.

Why It Works

The years that
shape the adult.

8 to 12 is when habits stick. Our class is built to make discipline, focus, and self-respect feel as natural as breathing — without ever feeling like a lecture.

01

Gi & No-Gi.

Both training modes — Gi for the technical foundation, No-Gi for the modern game. Plain white Gi for Gi class. Rash guard and shorts for No-Gi.

02

Self-defense that works.

Real grappling fundamentals — control, escapes, distance management. The kind of Jiu-Jitsu that handles real situations, not the make-believe kind.

03

Discipline that lasts.

Show up on time. Bow on the mat. Listen to the coach. Help your partner. The mat habits become school habits — and parents notice within months.

04

Black-belt led.

Every class is led or supervised by a black belt under Tom DeBlass. Five black belts on staff — not one of those programs where the kids' class is an afterthought.

05

Optional competition.

For kids who want to test themselves, we have a competition pathway — local tournaments, NAGA, IBJJF Kids. Always optional, never required.

06

Free first class.

$110/mo unlimited youth. No contract. Family discounts. Try a class before you decide — your child is the one who has to want to come back.
