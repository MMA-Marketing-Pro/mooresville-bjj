Your first class is free.

Walk in. Tap out. Walk out a different person. No commitment, no pressure, no card on file. We have a proven system to make sure anyone feels comfortable on the mat after a few minutes.

What Happens Next

Three steps. That's it.

1. Click the button below and tell us your name, email, and which class you want to try.

2. Pick a time on the calendar that works for you. We'll text confirmation and what to bring.

3. Walk into 243 Overhill Dr 10 minutes before your class. An instructor will greet you, partner you with a seasoned student, and walk you through warm-up + a few new moves. You will never be asked to do anything you aren't comfortable with.
